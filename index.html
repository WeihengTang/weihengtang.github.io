Learning Diffusion Model from Noisy Measurement using Principled Expectation-Maximization Method
Weimin Bai, Weiheng Tang, Enze Ye, Siyi Chen, Wenzheng Chen, He Sun
arXiv, 2024

We propose a principled EM framework for learning clean diffusion models from noisy measurements with arbitrary corruption types, enhancing the E-step of EMDiffusion by upgrading the DPS algorithm to a plug-and-play Monte Carlo (PMC) method.
